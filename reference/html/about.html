About us


Welcome to Amara Sensation Accessories, where elegance meets everyday style.

We are a Qatar-based fashion brand created with a simple goal: to make beautiful accessories and jewelry accessible to everyone — no matter their budget or style. Whether you're dressing up for a special event or just want a little sparkle in your day, Amara Bijoux is your destination for fashion-forward pieces that inspire confidence and self-expression.

🌟 What We Offer


Our collections include:

  • Stylish earrings, necklaces, bracelets, and rings

  • Trendy and timeless designs for all tastes

  • Affordable options without compromising on quality

  • Statement pieces and subtle everyday essentials


👑 Fashion for All


We believe fashion should be inclusive. That’s why we offer products for both low and high budgets, ensuring that everyone can find something they love. Every piece we offer is handpicked with care to reflect the latest trends, timeless elegance, and the individuality of our customers.


💬 Why Choose Us


  • Passionate about quality and design

  • Curated collections with a personal touch

  • Reliable service with delivery across Qatar

  • Cash on delivery available for your convenience


✨ Our Vision


To become one of Qatar’s most loved fashion brands — a go-to name for trendy, affordable, and premium accessories that speak to every personality and style.

Thank you for choosing Amara Sensation Accessories. Your trust and support mean everything to us.
